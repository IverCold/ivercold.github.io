Numenera 2.5 Salvaging System

Salvaging Process

Salvaging Numenera is series of Intellect based Tasks: initial task (am I able to gain anything here?) and task for every kind of Iotum (am I able to extract exactly what I want?). Tasks difficulty may be decreased by Salvaging Numenera skill, assets and efforts. Calculation of initial task duration goes according to the following rule: 15 minutes * assessed task difficulty. Plus you have to spend 5 more minutes for extracting every kind of Iotum.

Solving salvaging tasks series has the following order:

  1. Perform initial salvaging task. Success leads you to the second step. In case of failure there is no Iotum to extract.
  2. Calculate amount of Iotum kinds you want extract. Maximum level of available Iotum kind is equal to the base initial task difficulty.
    • Every Salvaging Numenera skill level produces one kind of Iotum.
    • Every asset produces one kind of Iotum.
  3. Every Iotum kind requires performing separate salvaging task. Task difficulty is equal to the Iotum level. Success grants amount of Iotum according to the column "Units Salvaged" in Iotum Salvaging table and some parts (extracted Iotum amount * Iotum level).
  4. Success in solving initial salvage task allows not only extract Iotum but also get some cyphers. Amount of cyphers is equal to the 1d6 - amount of Iotum kinds that you tried to extract.
