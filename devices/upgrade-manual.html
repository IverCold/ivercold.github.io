Numenera 2.5 Devices Upgrade Manual

Upgrading cyphers on the fly

This page was made by comparison of cyphers in the Numenera Corebook and Numenera Discovery books. It helps to upgrade cyphers from Technology Compendium on the fly. It uses cyphers as examples. Artefacts can be upgraded in the same way.

General fixes

  1. Numenera Lore → Understanding Numenera
  2. Ranges: 6.1m → 6m, 9.1m → 9m
  3. Same level or lower → Cypher level or lower
  4. Modified by one step → Eased or hindered
  5. Range of 200 feet [61 m] → Very long range

Duration of 28 hours almost always remains unchanged.

MAX is calculated as the maximum level that you can get from the dice roll. For example, for 1d6+2 cypher, MAX is 8, MAX-2 is 6.

Examples:

Antivenom Renders user immune to poisons of the same level or lower Renders user immune to poisons of the same level or lower for one hour per cypher level

Static duration → Set duration per cypher level

The lower border should be equal (or around) old duration. If there is some value (damage or armor) AND duration, then duration (not value) is increased by cypher level. Examples:

Tech Name Old Fix
Adhesion Clamps Lasts for twenty minutes. Lasts for ten minutes per cypher level.
Catseye Lasts for eight hours Lasts five hours per cypher level
Detonation (Flash) [powerful] For one minute Ten minutes if the cypher is level MAX-4 or higher
Eagleseye ability to see ten times as far as normal for one hour ability to see ten times as far as normal for one hour per cypher level
Force Screen Projector plane of solid force up to 20 feet by 20 feet (6.1 m by 6.1 m) for one hour. plane of solid force up to 20 feet by 20 feet (6 m by 6 m) for one hour per cypher level.
Magnetic Master The connection lasts for ten rounds. The connection lasts for ten rounds per cypher level.
Phase Changer [powerful] For one minute  Add: Two minutes if the cypher is level MAX-1 or higher

Damage → Increase damage for MAX-2 and MAX level

Takes 1 additional point of damage → Add: 2 points of the cypher if level MAX-2 or higher, 3 points of MAX level

Examples: Density Nodule, Disrupting Nodule, Heat Nodule, Retaliation Nodule, Shock Nodule


Armor → Increase armor for MAX-1 level

Examples:

Tech Name Old Fix
Force Nodule Adding 2 points to the armor it provides Add: adding 3 to the armor if the cypher is level MAX-1 or higher
Sheen Resists damage +1 to Armor Add: +2 to Armor if the cypher is level MAX-1 or higher

Range → Increase range for MAX-2 level

Close → Long range → Very long range. Examples:

Tech Name Old Fix
Attractor Long range Very long range if the cypher level MAX-2 or higher
Image Projector The image appears 25 feet away from the user The image appears up to a close distance away (long distance if the cypher level is MAX-2 or higher, very long distance if the cypher level is 6 or higher)
Ray Emitter (Numbing) Project a ray of energy up to 200 feet (61 m) Add: Very long range if the cypher is level MAX-2 or higher
Tracer Within short range Add: Long range if the cypher is level MAX-2 or higher, very long range if the cypher is level MAX or higher

Value → Increase it for MAX-2 level

Examples:

Datasphere Siphon One question Add: Two questions if the cypher is level MAX-2 or higher, three questions if MAX or higher
Gravity-Nullifying Spray A nonliving object about the size of a person or smaller Add: Two humans if the cypher is level 6 or higher
Intellect Enhancement Adds 1 to Intellect Edge for one hour Add: Or adds 2 if the cypher is level MAX-3 or higher
Knowledge Enhancement  The character has training in a predetermined skill Add: Or two skills if the cypher is level 5 or higher
Personal Environment Field  The aura extends to 1 foot (30 cm) around the user Add: double that radius if the cypher is level 7 or higher
Psychic Communique Telepathic message of up to ten words ten words per cypher level
